Research Notes

Welcome to my graduate research website. I am in my second year of graduate school at The Ohio State University. I am currently pre-candidacy and advised by Dr. Chris Orban. My current project focuses on developing a kHz rep rate data aquisition system for the Extreme Light Lab at the Wright Patterson Air Force Base. Currently we have kHz DAQ for the laser diodes and 10 Hz DAQ for the spectrometers. We are currently working to increase that rep rate, implement new parameters, and create a optimization feedback loop.
